How It Works

Up and running in under 5 minutes.

1

Open & Use

Open the app in any web browser — or install it to your home screen as an app. No download or setup needed.

2

Add Your Physicians

Start by adding your doctors. Their names will auto-fill when you create appointments and medications.

3

Add Family Members

Use the patient switcher to add family members. Each person gets their own separate medical records.

4

Save Your Data

Hit "Save My Data" to export a backup file anytime. Load it back in to restore. Your data is always yours.
